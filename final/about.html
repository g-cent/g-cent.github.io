About Us

In 2023, we set out on a journey to create something meaningful and transformative—MyPoint. As co-founders, Michael Russo and Gio Centofanti poured their hearts and souls into building a platform that would truly make a difference. Our story is one of grit, innovation, and countless late nights fueled by a shared vision. Michael brought his entrepreneurial spirit and leadership to the table, while Gio, with his unmatched technical expertise, turned complex ideas into seamless software solutions. Together, we navigated challenges, questioned conventional limits, and built a product we are proud to call ours. From brainstorming sessions that stretched until sunrise to relentless problem-solving, every ounce of effort was worth it. Those long nights and hard-fought battles taught us resilience and brought us closer to realizing our vision for MyPoint. Today, MyPoint stands as a testament to what’s possible when passion meets purpose. It’s more than a platform—it’s a movement, and we’re just getting started.
